Emerging Spatial Professionals

A group for emerging professionals in the spatial industry
with the purpose to network and advance our careers

  • Connect

Goal 1

Networking

To host regular events that provide networking opportunities for our membership to present their ideas, projects, and opinions to their peers

Goal 2

Engagement

To engage with the spatial industry, recent graduates, and educators to facilitate job postings, networking opportunities, internships, and volunteer opportunities

Goal 3

Encouragement

To encourage the New Zealand geospatial industry to provide support to geospatial professionals in the early years of their careers.
